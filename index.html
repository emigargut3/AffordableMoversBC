Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your move.

Local moves in the Metro Vancouver area typically range from $149–$189/hour depending on crew size and truck, with a 2–4 hour minimum. A 1-bedroom move usually totals $600–$900, and a 2-bedroom move $900–$1,500. We provide an exact quote upfront so you always know what to expect.
Yes! We offer professional packing services using high-quality boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, and mattress covers. You can also choose to pack yourself and just hire us for loading and transport. Packing materials are available for purchase if you'd like to handle your own packing.
Yes, we're fully licensed and insured. In the rare event of accidental damage, we have a clear, straightforward claims process. We also protect your walls, floors, and door frames during the move as standard practice — prevention is always our first priority.
Absolutely. We specialize in moving pianos, safes, pool tables, hot tubs, antiques, and other high-value or heavy specialty items. Our crew uses proper equipment and techniques specific to each item type. We recommend mentioning specialty items when you request your quote so we can plan accordingly.
Yes! We understand that life happens. We offer same-day and next-day moving services when availability permits. Call us directly at 604-537-4140 for urgent bookings — we'll do our best to accommodate you, 7 days a week.
After your move, leave us an honest review on Google Maps. Simply mention the review (or show it to our team) and we'll take $20 off your current move invoice. It's our way of saying thank you for helping other customers find us.
Moving services in BC are exempt from the 7% Provincial Sales Tax (PST). You only pay 5% GST on your move — which means you save compared to most other services. We include this clearly on every invoice.
Yes, we operate 7 days a week including most holidays. Weekend and end-of-month slots fill up fast, especially during summer (May–September). We recommend booking at least 1–2 weeks in advance for peak-season dates.
